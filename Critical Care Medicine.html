Critical Care Medicine

The department is led by two full-time critical care consultants and highly experienced team of resident doctors.

The state of the art modular ICCU/ITU is one of its kind and has the facilities as mentioned below:

  • All the beds are connected with 6 channel bed side monitors

  • A central monitoring system is always connected with all the beds which contains multi-parameter recording facilities

  • Invasive (most advanced ventilator with advanced modalities) and non-invasive ventilator facilities round the clock for every patient

  • In house facilities for haemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis and plasmapheresis

  • Advanced haemodynamic monitoring system (PICO)

  • Facilities for IABP available round the clock

  • Facilities for ultrasound, echocardiography and various other examinations are available 24x7

  • Provisions for temporary and permanent pacemaker implantation

  • The unit also contains isolated cabins with negative pressure gradient for infected patients
